App Design

Students were asked to create and design a concept for a mobile app that should solve a problem. My app, Clever Auto, was created to provide digestible information on vehicle maintenance for all users. Vehicle maintenance can initially seem overwhelming to most people, so the goal of Clever Auto is to be easily navigated, informative, and provide users with easy to follow tutorials and resources while maintaining a clean and simple aesthetic.
